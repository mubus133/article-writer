Crescent University Unveils N500m Proposed Medical Laboratory

…… As Kano Govt. Sponsors students for PG Programmes
Crescent University has unveiled a N500 million proposed medical laboratory centre donated by the Abdul Samad Rabiu (ASR) Africa Initiative. The Proprietor, Prince Muhammed Maruf Adesegun Ajibola, SAN, led the management of the university and top officials of the ASR Africa initiative to the groundbreaking ceremony. In his welcoming address, Prince Ajibola commended the ASR Africa initiative for the donation stating that students would be the biggest beneficiaries of the gesture.
